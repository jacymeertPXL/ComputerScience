Why I Chose This Education Program

I chose this education program because I feel a deep connection to it. It stems from my personal interests and the way I approach life, as well as what I studied in high school. Computer science and information technology feel like second nature to me.

I'm also highly passionate about creating and expressing my creativity, which I believe this education can offer me. From a young age, I worked on small coding projects, and I've always been driven to advance my skills in creation. I've aspired to be an engineer from a very young age, and later in my life, during high school, I decided on software engineering.

Moreover, given my physical condition due to a chronic illness, an education like this appears to be the most suitable choice.

The reason I specifically chose this school and program is because I've heard from family and friends that it's one of the best schools in Limburg, and they have an excellent support system for individuals with dyslexia, which I also have.
